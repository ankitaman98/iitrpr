Research and Innovation

IIT Ropar has made serious efforts to align its R&D focus with the intent of making a positive imapct on the world. We attract some of the best students and faculty that conduct research projects in advanced areas of science and engineering. The Institute has ongoing academic and research collaborations with many national and international universities, in order to keep pace with expanding frontiers of knowledge.

INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS

The Office of International Relations at IIT Ropar has been conceived keeping in mind the growing need for internationalization of Indian academic institutes and universities. IIT Ropar being a millennium university aims to leapfrog its global outreach and footprint by way of inter-university academic alliances through this office.
